Please read and accept before entering AdSeeker

1. Platform Use

AdSeeker is a B2B advertising matchmaking platform. By entering, you agree to use AdSeeker exclusively for legitimate business advertising partnerships.

2. Communication Monitoring

AdSeeker has the right to monitor and review all messages exchanged on the platform. This is to ensure compliance with our policies, prevent fraud, and maintain a safe environment for all users.

3. No Off-Platform Dealing

Users are strictly prohibited from bypassing the platform to complete deals externally. This includes:

  • Sharing personal email addresses, phone numbers, or social media handles in chat before a deal is confirmed
  • Arranging payments outside of AdSeeker's payment system
  • Soliciting or accepting direct contact from other users off-platform

4. Commission & Payments

AdSeeker charges a 5% commission fee on all transactions processed through the platform. All payments must be made through AdSeeker. Any attempt to avoid platform fees may result in immediate account suspension or legal action.

5. Deal Completion

All agreements initiated on AdSeeker must be completed through the platform. Contact details are only revealed after a confirmed agreement and payment has been processed.

6. Account Suspension

AdSeeker reserves the right to suspend or permanently ban any account found to be in violation of these terms. Repeated violations may result in legal action.

7. Dispute Resolution

All disputes between advertisers and publishers must be reported through the AdSeeker platform. Our admin team will review and mediate all reported disputes.
